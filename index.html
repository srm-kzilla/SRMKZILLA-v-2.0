Events.

SRMKZILLA regularly organises workshops, technical talks and discussions, meetups to spread the message of the power of open source to fellow students. We also organize Mozofest - a free for all access fest where we call in speakers and experts from the community to teach about the latest developments in open source and we also organise games and coding events where people can test their skill.
